> A 40-year-old Japanese man sitting at a desk in a modern university room, looking at a laptop screen showing an online video meeting with several people. Cherry blossom planning documents and a course registration form for "Mechanics" are spread on the desk. A whisky glass sits nearby. The window shows a spring campus with cherry trees beginning to bud. Warm afternoon light. Nostalgic yet forward-looking atmosphere. Anime-style illustration, soft lighting.
